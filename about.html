Our Story

Stage Fright began during the 2020 pandemic to bring joy to people in troubling times. Since they began during the lockdown, they have only been able to have in person concerts for long. Before then, Stage Fright became semi-YouTube famous posting both music videos and funny interactions between the band members. The band is still gaining, with its slow and steady rise. While it is still mainly considered to be an underground band, we at Stage Fright hope to gain mainstream attention one day.

Stage Fright as a band is well loved by its fans, despite its size. Stage Fright has won many awards for small competitions for their interesting take on rock music. Stage Fright writes all of its own music, and is always a hit whatever the song may be. Our biggest song "Creepers" was a smash hit for our small but mighty fanbase.

One of the things that sets Stage Fright apart is their commitment to authenticity. Every song they release is a reflection of their personal experiences, emotions, and the world around them. Their music blends classic rock influences with a modern twist, creating a unique sound that resonates with listeners of all ages. Whether it’s an energetic anthem or a slow, emotional ballad, Stage Fright’s songs are always crafted with passion and purpose, making each performance a memorable experience.

Beyond their music, Stage Fright is known for their strong connection with their fans. Through interactive livestreams, behind-the-scenes content, and engaging social media posts, the band makes an effort to stay connected with their growing audience. Fans appreciate not only their music but also their humor and genuine personalities. As they continue to release new songs and embark on future tours, Stage Fright remains dedicated to their mission of bringing people together through music, proving that even a small band can make a big impact.

Meet the Band

Anthony Youssef is the lead singer of our band stage fright, and face of the band. Ever since he was a kid, he has loved to sing. His parents told him how good his singing voice was, but he never believed them until everyone started complementing his voice. Only then did he realize that my true potential in life would be as lead singer for the band.

Luke Rinas leads the band with his steady drum beat. Luke loves drumming so much, that his hearing has started to go. That doesn't stop him from having the time of his life up on stage with his fellow band members in the greatest rock band ever, Stage Fright. Make sure to catch us on tour this year 2025.

Jack Steel is our main guitarist, leads the instrumentals with his creative rhythms. Jack has always loved the guitar in any form, whether it be an acoustic guitar or even a ukulele. He loves anything with a string that makes noise. The only thing he loves more than string instruments is the rock band and eating lollipops.

Sam Lombardo provides amazing chords and solos with his amazing keyboard playing. Sam had a rocky beginning in his musical career, with his limited talent in music. However, Sam kept practicing day after day and made up for his shortcomings. Nowadays, Sam is one of the most talented keyboard players in the planet. Some even claim that he is the greatest of all time.
